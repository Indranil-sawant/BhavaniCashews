Our Story

For over forty years, Bhavani Cashews has stood as a beacon of artisanal excellence in cashew processing. Established in 1978, our journey began with a simple yet uncompromising vision: to bring the finest cashew kernels to discerning tables worldwide.

auto_awesome

Traditional Roasting

Our kernels are slow-roasted in small, controlled batches, unlocking deep, buttery golden profiles that modern flash-roasting simply cannot replicate.

handshake

Direct Sourcing

We work directly with growers to ensure fair wages and premium harvests, establishing ethical, sustainable partnerships across decades.

Every cashew kernel we pack is hand-sorted and graded by skilled artisans who have perfected their craft across generations. This meticulous attention to detail ensures unrivaled size, ivory color purity, and that signature, luxurious crisp crunch.
