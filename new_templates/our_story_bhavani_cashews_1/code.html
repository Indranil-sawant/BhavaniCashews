A Note From Our Founder

It started with a simple observation: the world was moving too fast, and the soulful quality of hand-sorted cashews was being lost to industrial noise. In 1978, I set out to protect that soul.

At Bhavani Cashews, we don't just export nuts; we share a piece of our heritage. Each cashew is a testament to the patient hands that harvested it and the rigorous standards that define us.

Decades later, my promise remains the same: we will never compromise the artisanal process for the sake of volume. Your trust is our most valuable harvest.

A. Bhavani Shankar

The Journey of Purity

Trace the meticulous steps that transform a raw harvest into a Bhavani signature experience.

Step 01

Sustainable Harvest

Our farmers hand-pick only the ripest cashew apples from groves that have flourished for generations.

Step 02

Artisanal Grading

Each kernel is visually inspected for size, color, and texture by specialists with decades of experience.

Step 03

Purity Testing

Beyond the visual, our lab ensures every batch meets international safety and nutritional excellence benchmarks.

Step 04

Eco-Conscious Seal

Sealed in artisanal, biodegradable packaging to preserve crunch and flavor while respecting the earth.
